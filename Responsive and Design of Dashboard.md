"Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau"

This activity focuses on designing a "responsive, interactive, and well-structured Tableau dashboard" for the project **"Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau."** The dashboard integrates multiple visualizations, KPI cards, and interactive filters into a clean and user-friendly interface, making cosmetic market data easy to explore and understand.

The dashboard features consistent color schemes, proper alignment, and organized spacing to enhance readability and provide an appealing visual experience. Interactive filters, including **Brand, Product Category, Skin Type, and Price Range**, allow users to dynamically analyze the dataset based on their specific interests.

The responsive layout enables stakeholders to compare products, evaluate pricing trends, examine brand performance, and identify consumer preferences efficiently. Overall, the dashboard transforms complex cosmetic data into meaningful visual insights, improving usability and supporting faster, data-driven decision-making using Tableau.
