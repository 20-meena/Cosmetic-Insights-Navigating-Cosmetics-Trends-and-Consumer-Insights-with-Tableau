**Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau**

This activity demonstrates the use of **multiple Tableau Story Points** to present the analysis of the **"Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau"** project in a clear, structured, and sequential format. Each story point highlights a specific aspect of the cosmetics dataset, including **KPI dashboards, brand performance, product categories, pricing trends, skin-type coverage, and consumer insights**.

The story progresses logically from an overall market overview to detailed analyses, enabling users to understand the relationships between different metrics and identify key market trends. By combining interactive dashboards and visualizations into a single narrative, the project provides a comprehensive view of the cosmetics industry.

Overall, the Tableau Story enhances data storytelling by transforming complex cosmetic data into meaningful business insights, making the analysis more engaging, easier to interpret, and effective for informed decision-making.
