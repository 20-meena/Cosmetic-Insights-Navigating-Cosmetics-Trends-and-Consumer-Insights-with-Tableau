
## Number of Visualizations

The **"Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau"** project consists of **eight interactive Tableau visualizations**, each designed to analyze a specific aspect of the cosmetics dataset and provide meaningful business insights.

The visualizations included in the project are:

1. **KPI Dashboard** – Displays key metrics such as Total Products, Total Brands, Average Price, and Average Rank.
2. **Category-Level Product Distribution Analysis** – Shows the distribution of cosmetic products across different product categories.
3. **Top 10 Brands by Product Count** – Identifies the brands offering the highest number of cosmetic products.
4. **Average Price by Brand** – Compares the average prices of products across different cosmetic brands.
5. **Skin Type Product Distribution** – Analyzes the number of products available for Dry, Oily, Combination, Normal, and Sensitive skin types.
6. **Brand Performance Analysis** – Highlights brand-wise product availability and overall market presence.
7. **Product Rating Analysis** – Examines the distribution of product rankings and customer ratings across brands and categories.
8. **Category and Brand Insights Dashboard** – Combines category performance, brand comparison, and pricing trends into an interactive analytical view.

Together, these visualizations provide a comprehensive understanding of cosmetic market trends, brand competition, pricing strategies, product distribution, and consumer preferences. The combination of dashboards and interactive charts enables users to explore the dataset efficiently and make informed business decisions.
