## Utilization of Data Filters

Multiple interactive filters were implemented across the **"Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau"** dashboard to enable dynamic exploration and detailed analysis of the cosmetics dataset. These filters allow users to customize the visualizations and focus on specific product segments based on their analytical needs.

The following interactive filters were implemented:

- **Brand Filter:** Allows users to select one or more cosmetic brands to compare product availability, pricing, and performance.
- **Product Category Filter:** Enables users to view products belonging to specific categories such as skincare, makeup, haircare, and others for focused analysis.
- **Skin Type Filter:** Allows users to analyze products suitable for **Dry, Oily, Combination, Normal, and Sensitive** skin types.
- **Price Range Filter:** Helps users explore products within a selected price range to understand pricing patterns and market segmentation.
- **Product Rank Filter:** Enables users to filter products based on their rankings and identify highly rated cosmetics.

A **Category (Label) Filter** is prominently used throughout the dashboard to allow users to dynamically select and analyze specific cosmetic categories. This interactive filter updates all related visualizations instantly, making it easier to compare product distribution, brand performance, pricing trends, and customer preferences within the selected category.

These interactive filters enhance dashboard usability by providing flexible data exploration, enabling users to discover meaningful insights and make informed decisions based on their specific areas of interest
